# TripMatch 🌍

An interactive guide to find your perfect travel destination.

Filter 40+ countries by month, climate, safety level, and travel style 
— nature, art, culture, or relaxation — and get detailed, honest 
information.

## What's inside each country

- **Safety, health & infrastructure** — in-depth descriptions, not just ratings
- **4 experience categories** — Nature, Art, Culture, Relax.
- Each with curated activities, non-obvious tips, and tags
- **Month-by-month calendar** — climate, local festivals, estimated crowd levels

## Why it exists

Choosing a destination usually means hours of research — 
ministry of foreign affairs sites, travel blogs, forums, 
Facebook groups full of strangers' opinions — and by the time 
you've finished planning, you're already exhausted. And still second-guessing.

Or there's a cheap flight and you just want to know: is it worth it?

I built this for myself, to create a bucket list of destinations 
to explore with my kids. For each country, I used AI to aggregate 
hundreds of sources — safety reports, health infrastructure, climate data, 
local festivals, crowd patterns — then verified everything manually.

The result is the kind of overview that would take weeks to compile alone.
I'm sharing it because it shouldn't be that hard.

You'll see there are wonders everywhere. Whatever your budget or time, 
there's always somewhere worth going. **Travel is my greatest passion, and this is my way of sharing it**.

*Next step: a tool that won't just help you choose where to go, 
but plans the entire trip.* 😉
